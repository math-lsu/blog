Resume

  • Higher Education

  • Work Experience

    • 1984-1987 Math Instructor, Universidad de los Andes. Mérida, Venezuela.
    • 1987-1990 Assistant Professor, Universidad del Zulia. Maracaibo, Venezuela.
    • 1990-1995 Teaching Assistant, Louisiana State University. Baton Rouge, USA.
    • 1995-2004 Full Math Professor, Universidad del Zulia. Maracaibo, Venezuela.
    • 2004-2005 Visiting Professor, Louisiana State University. Baton Rouge, USA.
    • 2005-2009 Full Math Professor, Universidad del Zulia. Maracaibo, Venezuela.
    • 2016 - 2024 Professor Emeritus, Universidad del Zulia. Maracaibo, Venezuela.

    I taught the following courses at university level: Calculus at all levels, Algebra, Lineal Algebra, Commutative Algebra, Abstract Algebra at all levels, Real and Complex Analysis, Topology and Algebraic Topology, Geometry and Differential Geometry, Lie Groups and Lie Algebras and many other elective courses. I also worked as a thesis advisor of several math students in the school of sciences of Universidad del Zulia in Maracaibo, Venezuela.

    From 1997 to 1999 I worked as a Director of the Mathematics Department from The School of Science of la Universidad del Zulia and from 1999 to 2003 I worked as a Director of Graduate Studies of the same School.

    I used the following computer tools as teaching resources: Latex, Mathematica, Matlab, Maplex, Python, Linux Ubuntu, Power Point, and others.

    Over the last several years, I have been involved in software development projects carried out in-house that manages student enrollment and courses administration, bloggin, ecommerce and general programmming mainly using Python, Django, Pandas, Matplotlib, Numpy, and javascript as well as HTML, CSS, git, GitHub and Heroku for deployment.
